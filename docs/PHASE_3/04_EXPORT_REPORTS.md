# Feature 3.4: Export & Reports

**Feature Name:** Data Export & Report Generation  
**Status:** ⏳ Not Started  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Estimated Time:** 1 day

---

## 📝 Description

Enable users to export analytics and lead data:
- Export leads to CSV
- Export analytics reports
- Schedule email reports
- Generate PDF reports
- Segment leads by criteria

---

## ✅ Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Leads can be exported to CSV
- [ ] Analytics can be exported
- [ ] Exports are formatted correctly
- [ ] Reports can be scheduled
- [ ] Email delivery works
- [ ] Large exports handled efficiently

---

## 📋 Export Types

1. **Lead Export**
   - CSV format
   - All form fields
   - Submission date
   - Referrer information
   - Download status

2. **Analytics Report**
   - Date range
   - Whitepaper selection
   - PDF or CSV
   - Charts included (for PDF)

3. **Scheduled Reports**
   - Weekly summary
   - Monthly summary
   - Custom schedule
   - Email delivery

Create:
